Face to face class mode Dashboard

To start using Facecheck's face to face class mode:
Step 1: you need to add all your student's information to the system.
   *To add student, click your photo in the upper right corner and then choose the 'Add Student' option.
    *To edit the student's information, you can choose the 'Update/View Student Information' option.
Step 2: Start taking attendance using face recognition.
   *To start taking attedance, choose 'Take Attendance' option.
Step 3: View the attendance list.
    *To see the list of attendance, choose 'View Attendance List' option.
   *To view and edit your profile, click your photo in the upper right corner and then choose the 'Profile' option.
To go back to online class mode, you can click the 'Online Class Mode' option.
